    Welcome to Landgasthof Spessartruh in Steiger

    In the heart of the Spessart forest, you will find a traditional inn with polished wooden tables, a cozy tiled stove, and hearty dishes.
    Enjoy regional cuisine that uses the best products from our own breeding and production.
    Our homemade sausages and ham are prepared deliciously and hearty by master butcher Georg Gerber.
    From snacks to juicy steaks, delicious salads, and vegetarian options, the menu leaves little to be desired.
    Perhaps you can also try a glass of homemade „Äppelwoi“ (apple wine) or enjoy a freshly tapped beer from the region.

    Steiger and Surroundings

    The small village of Steiger, with around 80 inhabitants, is located on an elevated plateau without through traffic. The fresh air of the Spessart region and the peaceful, relaxing location are perfect for extensive hikes, cycling, and motorbike tours.
