Welcome to Landgasthof Spessartruh in Steiger

Nestled in the heart of the Spessart forest, you’ll find a traditional tavern with polished wooden tables, a cozy tiled stove, and a panoramic beer garden.
Look forward to regional cuisine with hearty dishes. From classic snacks (Vesper) to juicy steaks, tasty salads, and vegetarian options — the menu leaves little to be desired.
Perhaps you’ll try a glass of our local Äppelwoi (apple wine) or enjoy a freshly tapped beer from the region.

Steiger and Surroundings

The small village of Steiger, with around 80 inhabitants, is located on an elevated plateau without through traffic. The fresh air of the Spessart region and the peaceful, relaxing location are perfect for extensive hikes, cycling, and motorbike tours.
